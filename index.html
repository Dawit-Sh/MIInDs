MIInDs-Ethiopia is a podcast featuring the Tech-Savvy Dawit Sharon and Philomath Abraham Mengstu along side the parapsychologist Yafet Yared, who share a love for ethics, pop culture, and cognitive science, and who have a marked inability to distinguish sacred from profane. Each podcast includes discussions of moral philosophy, recent work on moral psychology and neuroscience, and the overlap between the two.

We also include a variety of topics in our episodes, because defining ourselves with philosophy and psychology will be only to limit. So we also raise different topics like Biographies,Technology,History, Random Rants and what not. Our main purpose is to reshape the current state of Ethiopia into a better one combined with Ethiopia's Great past, we want to build a better Tomorrow
